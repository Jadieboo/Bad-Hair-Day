About Us

I started as a mobile hair dresser and after building a client list I opened my first studio back in 2011 with the help and support of my family and now we have 400 studios around the west midlands. We are a family run busines, which we're very proud of and because of this, every time you visit one of our studios we aim to make you feel welcome as if you are around family!

During a visit to one of our studios not only will you recieve a professional service using the best products in the industry, you will also be free to engage in conversation or sit peacefully in some much needed time to yourself. We have individual pods for each client, with a seat for a friend to tag along. No more waiting outside! So what are you waiting for? Choose your preferred experience and book today!
